Methodology

Every engagement follows a structured four-phase framework grounded in Lean Six Sigma’s DMAIC model.

01

Discover

Comprehensive operational assessment. We map every workflow, conduct stakeholder interviews, and produce a Current State Assessment with a prioritized gap analysis.

02

Design

Target-state architecture. SOPs, process maps, hiring frameworks, onboarding materials, and digital infrastructure, documented and ready for deployment. Deliverable: Operational Systems Blueprint.

03

Deploy

Full implementation and team training. Every system goes live. Nothing remains conceptual. Deliverable: Deployed systems, training documentation, and transition support.

04

Sustain

Ongoing advisory and system optimization. Monthly reviews, process updates, and quarterly health assessments as the business evolves. Deliverable: Monthly operational reports.

The Bell Transferability Score

Our proprietary assessment framework.

Every business has a transferability score. It measures how effectively the operation runs without any single individual, including the owner.

We assess five dimensions: process documentation coverage, people system maturity, digital infrastructure readiness, knowledge centralization, and operational resilience. The resulting score identifies exactly where the gaps are and what it takes to close them.

Businesses with high transferability scores command higher valuations, retain employees longer, survive leadership transitions, and give their owners the ability to step back without the operation degrading.

The Discovery Engagement produces your score. The remaining phases close the gaps it identifies.
