Who We Help

Service business owners who have outgrown intuition-based operations and need structure to scale.

Bell Advisory Group works with home service businesses generating $500K to $5M+ in annual revenue that face one or more of the following challenges:

The owner is the bottleneck.

Every decision, every customer escalation, every new-hire question runs through one person. The business cannot function without the owner present. We build the process architecture that eliminates single-point-of-failure operations.

Nothing is documented.

Employees learn by shadowing. Quality varies by who is working. Onboarding takes months instead of weeks. We create complete SOP libraries and training documentation that make performance repeatable.

Hiring is a constant crisis.

You post on job boards, hope for the best, and lose half your hires within 90 days. We install structured hiring and retention systems that reduce mis-hires and cut time-to-productivity in half.

The business cannot sell.

73% of businesses listed for sale never close — almost always because operations depend on the owner. We build the operational infrastructure that makes your business transferable and commands a premium valuation.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bell Advisory Group?

Bell Advisory Group is an operations consulting firm that builds SOPs, hiring systems, and digital infrastructure for home service businesses. We apply Lean Six Sigma methodology to small and mid-size companies — HVAC, plumbing, pool service, pest control, electrical, and other skilled trades — to make their operations transferable, scalable, and independent of the owner.

What industries does Bell Advisory Group serve?

We serve home service and skilled trades businesses including HVAC, pool service, pest control, lawn care and landscaping, plumbing, electrical, cleaning, and restoration companies. Our methodology is industry-agnostic — documented operational systems follow the same principles regardless of vertical — but we focus on service businesses where owner dependency is the primary growth constraint.

What does an operations consultant do for a small business?

An operations consultant audits how your business actually runs — workflows, decision points, communication patterns, hiring practices — and builds the documented systems that allow it to function consistently without depending on any single person. At Bell Advisory Group, every engagement produces implemented deliverables: SOPs, hiring frameworks, onboarding programs, quality control systems, and digital infrastructure. We do not deliver slide decks or theoretical recommendations.

How much does operations consulting cost for a home service business?

Engagement scope and investment vary based on the size of your operation, the number of workflows to document, and which systems you need built. Every engagement begins with a conversation where we assess your current state, identify the highest-impact gaps, and outline a structured scope. We do not publish fixed pricing because every business has different needs, but we are transparent about investment during the scoping process.

What is the Bell Transferability Score?

The Bell Transferability Score is a structured diagnostic that evaluates five operational dimensions: process documentation, people system maturity, digital infrastructure, knowledge centralization, and operational resilience. It produces a quantified assessment of how dependent your business is on you — and exactly what it takes to close the gaps. This assessment is conducted during our Discovery engagement, not as a self-serve tool.

How is Bell Advisory Group different from other business consultants?

Three things set us apart. First, we build systems, not slide decks — every deliverable is implemented, tested, and documented for your team to maintain. Second, we specialize in the operational layer that most consultants skip: the SOPs, hiring frameworks, and process architecture that actually make a business run without the owner. Third, we apply Lean Six Sigma methodology — the same structured approach used by the largest consulting firms — adapted for the pace and constraints of growing service businesses.

Where is Bell Advisory Group located?

Bell Advisory Group is based in Central Florida and serves clients nationally. Our Discovery phase includes on-site assessment, and ongoing advisory work is conducted remotely with regular check-ins.
